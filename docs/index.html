Wallpaper Orchestrator

A desktop tool that organizes curated wallpaper collections, hosts them, and prepares them for publishing to Pinterest — fast, clean, and consistent.

See how it works View on GitHub

What it does

Wallpaper Orchestrator streamlines the workflow of preparing and publishing high-quality wallpaper collections to Pinterest.

🗂️

Organize by category

Sort your wallpapers into folders — Ads, Magazine, Posters, Art and more. Each folder becomes a Pinterest board.

🏷️

Smart descriptions

Automatically generates titles, descriptions and relevant hashtags for every image.

🔗

Hosted media

Images are hosted and turned into public links ready for Pinterest's bulk publishing.

📊

Bulk-ready export

Produces a ready-to-import CSV so you can publish dozens of pins at once.

How it works

Four simple steps from a folder of images to published pins.

Collect your images

Drop your wallpapers into category folders on your computer.

Build a pack

The app uploads the images, collects their public links, and generates a Pinterest-ready CSV with titles, descriptions and tags.

Publish to Pinterest

Import the CSV into Pinterest's bulk create tool — all pins are created on the right boards.

Clean up

Once published, remove the temporary hosted copies in one click.
