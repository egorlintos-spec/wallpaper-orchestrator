Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Wallpaper Orchestrator ("the App", "we", "us"), operated by Fallen the Owl, handles information when you use the application available at fallentheowl.space and its related tools.

1. Information We Collect

Wallpaper Orchestrator is a desktop application that runs on your own computer. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal data. Specifically:

  • Image files you select remain on your device and on hosting destinations you explicitly configure.
  • Access tokens (for example, GitHub or Pinterest) that you provide are stored locally on your machine and are used only to perform the actions you request.
  • We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or personal identifiers on our own infrastructure.

2. How We Use Information

Any credentials or tokens you supply are used solely to:

  • Upload images to a hosting location you choose;
  • Create or manage pins and boards on Pinterest on your behalf;
  • Generate publishing files (such as CSV exports) on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App can interact with third-party platforms that you choose to connect, including GitHub and Pinterest. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms. We recommend reviewing:

  • Pinterest Privacy Policy — policy.pinterest.com
  • GitHub Privacy Statement — docs.github.com

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is transmitted only to the services you explicitly connect, and only to carry out actions you initiate.

5. Data Retention

Because the App runs locally, you control all stored data. Tokens and files persist only on your device until you remove them. Images uploaded to a hosting destination can be deleted by you at any time through the App.

6. Security

Credentials are stored locally on your device. You are responsible for keeping your machine and access tokens secure. We recommend using tokens with the minimum necessary permissions.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at lotr1k@outlook.com.
